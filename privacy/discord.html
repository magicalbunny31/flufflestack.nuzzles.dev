privacy policy

  • here is how we use your data when you interact with the app so that it can provide you with its service
  • by using this app, you're agreeing to all the policies listed here

what personal data is collected?

  • when you interact with the app, your user id is collected and stored
    • this is used to differentiate your other data between other discord users who also use the app
  • if you interact with the app in a server, the server id and channel id are also collected and stored
    • this is used for storing server-specific other data, as well as providing you with metrics about your usage with the app

what other data is collected?

  • this is any data generated from the app by you or any data you input into the app, examples of this data are:
    • a user-defined reason for reporting a fox media in a modal
    • how many times you've viewed a specific fox media or used a command
    • your stored user settings
  • all apps can access a set of baseline data from discord, such as your discord username and avatar
    • this data is only used for formatting purposes and is not stored unless previously stated

how is this data used?

  • we use your personal data and other data to provide you with the app's services
  • no data is disclosed to any third parties
    • we may disclose your data when required to by discord in order to enforce their privacy policy
  • your data is kept until your discord account is deleted
    • usage data is anonymised (through stripping of personal data) and may be kept to help improve the app over time
    • we may retain your data for longer when required to by discord as stated in their retention policy
    • data is deleted from our live databases within 7 days of a request and is completely removed from all backups after a further 14 days
  • data is kept in encrypted databases that only the developers can access
  • as permitted by our legitimate interests, we may retain minimal data (such as your user id) even after a deletion request
    • this is used to enforce blacklists and maintain the security of our services

what rights do i have?

  • we'll update these policies from time to time for the app's service and to stay compliant with discord
    • you'll be told when these policies update and what was updated through using the app
  • you can request for a copy of your personal data and other data or for it to be deleted at any time - this can be done by:
    • requesting it through the app
    • joining the support server
  • if you have questions or concerns about this policy, you can contact the developers by:
    • using one of these external contact links
    • emailing hewwo@nuzzles.dev
